Exploring Global Suicide Rates: Insights from Power BI Analysis

This analysis delves into global suicide rates using Power BI to uncover trends, patterns, and regional disparities. Through interactive visualizations, the project sheds light on the complexities of this significant public health issue.

  • Data Analysis:
    • Line Graph Analysis: Tracks the temporal evolution of suicide rates to identify fluctuations and trends over time.
    • Mapping Suicide Rates: Provides a spatial perspective on geographical variations, highlighting hotspots.
    • Bar Chart Representation: Offers a comparative view of suicide counts by country/region to identify outliers.
  • Key Findings:
    • Identified significant regional disparities and temporal trends.
    • Uncovered correlations with socio-economic indicators.
  • Implications:
    • Informs targeted policy interventions and the development of early warning systems.
    • Emphasizes the need for cross-sector collaboration in suicide prevention.
  • Technologies Used:
    • Power BI
